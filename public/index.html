InstaGroom.Me

Application that serves as a information center for a independent Dog Groomer. InstaGroom.Me provides a full life cycle of a groomer's business to help them stay on top of their needs of clientele. With InstaGroom.Me, a Groomer can accomplish the following:
  • Input a new clients information for both the dog owner as well as the pet.
  • Provide real-time issues and needs for individual pets.
  • Schedule an appointment with a client while also providing services to be performed during the appointment
  • View appointments on a daily aspect
  • View the route on map from current location to an upcoming appointment's location
